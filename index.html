ABOUT US

Welcome to Vancouver Paving Company. Serving Vancouver, WA and the Greater Portland Area since 1988. We specialize in all phases of asphalt, from minor repairs to new construction. This also includes removal and replacement of parking lot, driveways, and roadways.

No paving project is too big for Vancouver Paving Company. We will be happy to make repairs to your existing driveway or develop and construct a new driveway to your standards. Our specialties also include parking lots, roadways, pathways, and playgrounds. Our professional staff provides excellent customer service from the beginning to the end of your project.

OUR SERVICES

We are knowledgeable in all aspects of the paving process that include drainage, slope, orientation, dimension, compaction and surface types. We provide professional advice to help you make expert choices for repairing, resurfacing or completing a new construction project. Our expertise in the field of paving along with our understanding of local, city and state governmental building codes, make Vancouver Paving the right choice for your next paving project.
